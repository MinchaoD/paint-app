About this App

Some features of this paint app are:
  • Users can draw lines with color changing from hue to be 0 to 360.
  • Users can choose the brush size with 3 options: thin, medium and thick.
  • Users can choose different stamps to add onto their drawings.
  • Users can erase their drawings by clicking on the eraser icon button.
  • Users can save their drawings by clicking the save icon button.
